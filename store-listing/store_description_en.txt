ZUGFeRD & E-Invoice Reader reads AND creates electronic invoices – and helps you manage them end to end: review incoming invoices, write your own, track payments, send payment reminders and get the numbers for your tax return. Ideal for freelancers and small businesses who want to handle their entire invoice management on the phone – fully local, no cloud.

READ INVOICES
• Peppol BIS 3.0 (UBL 2.1), XRechnung (UBL & CII), ZUGFeRD 1.x/2.x, Factur-X, UN/CEFACT CII, KSeF FA(3) incl. correction invoices
• Import as XML or hybrid PDF, multi-select, straight from email attachments
• Detail view with 3 tabs: readable invoice, raw XML with live search, original PDF
• Credit notes and corrections detected automatically, amounts with the correct sign

CREATE INVOICES (ZUGFeRD)
• Issue your own e-invoices and credit notes as hybrid ZUGFeRD PDF/A-3 (EN 16931)
• Company profile with logo, automatic invoice numbering, default payment terms
• Customer master with email addresses and an article catalog for recurring items – fill in invoices in seconds
• EN 16931 pre-flight check before generating, built-in self-test of the produced file
• Recurring invoices: duplicate any final invoice as a new draft

MANAGEMENT & PAYMENTS
• Paid status for incoming AND outgoing invoices
• Overdue documents at a glance: filters and coloured markers in the list
• Dunning for your customers: escalating payment reminders (levels 1–3) as a pre-filled email with the invoice PDF attached
• Due-date reminders: daily notifications for incoming invoices about to fall due and overdue outgoing invoices
• Notes & categories per document, search and date-range filters

DASHBOARDS & TAX
• Expense dashboard: payment status, expenses per month, top suppliers
• Revenue dashboard: revenue from your created invoices, open and overdue amounts, top customers
• VAT per quarter – the numbers for your VAT return at a glance

EXPORT & BACKUP
• CSV export (UTF-8) for your tax advisor, ZIP bundle with the original XML/PDF files by date range
• PDF export of individual documents
• Full backup as a single ZIP file: database, attachments, created invoices, company profile with logo and settings – perfect when switching devices

AI ASSISTANT (OPTIONAL)
• Add your own LLM provider: OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, Mistral, OpenRouter, Langdock or a generic OpenAI-compatible endpoint
• Ask questions about an open invoice right inside the document
• Note: only when you actively use the AI assistant is the data of that invoice sent to the provider you chose. Otherwise nothing leaves your device.

CONVENIENCE
• Dark mode, context-sensitive help on every screen
• Multilingual: German, English, Dutch, French and Polish
• Edge-to-edge design (Android 15 ready)

PRIVACY
All invoices are stored exclusively on your device. No cloud, no telemetry, no tracking. Data is only transmitted if you actively use the optional AI assistant – and then exclusively to the provider you selected.

NOTE
This is an independent hobby project and not an official PEPPOL or KSeF application. The information shown does not replace the original XML document.
