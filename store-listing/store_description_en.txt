E-Invoice & Peppol Reader imports and reads electronic invoices – Peppol BIS 3.0 (UBL), XRechnung, ZUGFeRD, Factur-X and the Polish KSeF FA(3). Ideal for freelancers, small businesses and anyone who wants to review, manage and forward their digital receipts to a tax advisor right from their phone.

SUPPORTED FORMATS
• Peppol BIS 3.0 (UBL 2.1)
• XRechnung – both the UBL and the CII profile
• ZUGFeRD 1.x and 2.x – hybrid PDFs with embedded XML
• Factur-X – hybrid PDF with XML
• UN/CEFACT Cross Industry Invoice (CII)
• KSeF FA(3) – Polish e-invoice format incl. correction invoices (KOR / KOR_ZAL / KOR_ROZ)

IMPORT
• XML and hybrid PDF files, multi-select supported
• Receive files directly from email attachments via the share function
• ZUGFeRD and Factur-X PDFs are detected automatically – the embedded XML is extracted and the original PDF is kept as an attachment

OVERVIEW & MANAGEMENT
• Clear invoice list with a colour-coded format badge per document
• Searchable by company or invoice number
• Filter chips: All / Unpaid / Overdue
• Date-range filter to focus on a specific period
• Mark each invoice as paid – including a marker in the list
• Due-date reminders: daily notifications with a configurable lead time (0 / 3 / 7 / 14 days)
• Credit notes and KSeF corrections are detected automatically and highlighted, amounts shown with the correct sign

DETAIL VIEW (3 TABS)
• Invoice – all relevant data, easy to read: supplier, recipient, line items, net, VAT, total, IBAN and payment information
• Raw data – the full XML with syntax highlighting and live search
• Original PDF – view it right inside the app, if attached

EXPORT
• PDF export – save or share an invoice as a readable PDF
• CSV export (UTF-8) for your tax advisor
• ZIP bundle with the CSV plus the original XML/PDF files, freely by date range

AI ASSISTANT (OPTIONAL)
• Add your own LLM provider: OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, Mistral, OpenRouter, Langdock or a generic OpenAI-compatible endpoint
• Paste your API key, load the models, pick a default
• Ask questions about an open invoice right inside the document
• Note: when you use the AI assistant, the data of that invoice is sent to the provider you chose. Without AI usage, nothing leaves your device.

DASHBOARD
• Visual breakdown of your expenses by month and supplier

CONVENIENCE
• Dark mode
• Multilingual: German, English, Dutch, French and Polish
• Edge-to-edge design (Android 15 ready)

PRIVACY
All invoices are stored exclusively on your device. No cloud, no telemetry, no tracking. Data is only transmitted if you actively use the optional AI assistant – and then exclusively to the provider you selected.

NOTE
This is an independent hobby project and not an official PEPPOL or KSeF application. The information shown does not replace the original XML document.
