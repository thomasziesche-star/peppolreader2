# Privacy Policy for Peppol Reader

**Last updated: February 21, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the Peppol Reader mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Peppol Reader is designed to function as a offline tool. 
- **Personal Data**: The app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- **Invoices**: All Peppol XML files and generated PDFs are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties by the app.
- **Data Deletion**: Since no data is collected on our servers, you are in full control of your data. Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

## 2. Permissions
The app may request the following permissions:
- **Internet Access**: Only used locally if required by internal libraries; no data is sent externally.
- **Storage/File Access**: Used to read Peppol XML files from your device and save generated PDFs.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app does not use any third-party services that collect data (e.g., Analytics, Ads).

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
